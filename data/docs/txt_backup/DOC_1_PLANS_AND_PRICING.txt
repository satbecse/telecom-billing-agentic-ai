SECTION 1: MOBILE PLAN TIERS
=============================================================================

BASIC MOBILE PLAN
-----------------
Monthly Base Price: $29.99/month
Data Allowance: 5 GB high-speed data (throttled to 512 Kbps after limit)
Voice: Unlimited domestic calls
Text: Unlimited domestic SMS/MMS
International: Not included (see add-ons)
Hotspot: Not available
Streaming Quality: SD (480p) only

PRO MOBILE PLAN
---------------
Monthly Base Price: $49.99/month
Data Allowance: 25 GB high-speed data (throttled to 1 Mbps after limit)
Voice: Unlimited domestic + Canada/Mexico calls
Text: Unlimited domestic + international SMS
International: 100 minutes included
Hotspot: 10 GB included
Streaming Quality: HD (1080p)
Priority Support: Yes

UNLIMITED ELITE PLAN
--------------------
Monthly Base Price: $79.99/month
Data Allowance: Truly unlimited high-speed data (no throttling)
Voice: Unlimited worldwide calls to 80+ countries
Text: Unlimited worldwide SMS/MMS
International: Unlimited to 80+ countries
Hotspot: 50 GB high-speed hotspot
Streaming Quality: 4K UHD
Priority Support: Yes
Premium Perks: Free streaming bundle (Netflix, Disney+, Hulu)

=============================================================================
SECTION 2: ADDITIONAL SERVICES AND ADD-ONS
=============================================================================

INTERNATIONAL CALLING ADD-ON
----------------------------
Price: $10.00/month
Includes: 500 minutes to 40+ countries

DEVICE PROTECTION PLAN
----------------------
Price: $12.99/month
Coverage: Accidental damage, theft, loss
Deductible: $99-$249 depending on device tier

PREMIUM VOICEMAIL
-----------------
Price: $4.99/month
Features: Visual voicemail, voicemail-to-email transcription

FAMILY LINE DISCOUNT
--------------------
Lines 2-4: $10 discount per line
Lines 5+: $15 discount per line
Maximum lines per account: 10

=============================================================================
SECTION 3: TAXES AND REGULATORY FEES
=============================================================================

The following fees are applied to all plans:
- Federal Universal Service Fee: $2.50/month
- State Telecommunications Tax: Varies by state (typically 4-8%)
- E911 Emergency Services Fee: $1.50/month
- Administrative Fee: $3.00/month

Note: All prices shown are before applicable taxes and fees.
